Ekleel Alward


Ekleel Alward is a charming and beautiful restaurant located in the Rafidia area of Nablus, Palestine. This stunning venue has become a popular spot for locals and visitors alike, thanks to its elegant design and multiple corners that are perfect for taking photos or creating reels. Whether you're looking for a cozy spot to enjoy a meal or a scenic backdrop for your social media content, Ekleel Alward offers a visually appealing ambiance that sets it apart.
The restaurant serves a special variety of food and desserts, offering a unique culinary experience for its guests. From traditional dishes to modern fusion cuisine, Ekleel Alward is known for its quality and flavor. The dessert menu is equally impressive, with sweet treats that are beautifully presented and perfect for satisfying any craving.
